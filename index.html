About Me

FUN: FACTS: I sing every morning and change the words of songs. Occasionally the words to the song make sense. I am a novice painter. I use recipes as guidelines, and it is not in my nature to follow a recipe without making alterations. I think well-made furniture is art. I like to restore unloved furniture, give it new life, and donate it to shelters. I have looked death in the face more than 3 times.
